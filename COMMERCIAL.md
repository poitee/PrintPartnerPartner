# Commercial licensing

Print Partner’s source code is under the **[Print Partner Non-Commercial Software License](LICENSE)**. That license covers everyday personal, academic, and non-commercial use, modification, and sharing.

This page is only about uses that **the license does not allow** without a separate written agreement with **Chad Lynch** (Print Partner).

---

## Usually fine without a commercial license

- Running Print Partner in your shop, studio, or **commercial print farm** to manage jobs, kits, plates, checkoff, and exports — **internal use only**
- Operating a **3D printing business** that sells printed parts, kits, or print services while using Print Partner on your own machines (**Commercial Print Farm Use** in [LICENSE](LICENSE))
- **Selling the physical parts** or print services you deliver (the license applies to the **software**, not your products)
- Personal, educational, hobby, and typical nonprofit use as described in [LICENSE-SUMMARY.md](LICENSE-SUMMARY.md)

Larger print operations that only **use** Print Partner internally do not need to contact Chad Lynch. Contact is for **commercializing the software itself** (see below).

---

## Needs written permission

Contact Chad Lynch before:

- Selling Print Partner or a fork (installer, app store, paid download, etc.)
- Bundling Print Partner inside another product you sell
- Offering Print Partner (or your derivative) as a **SaaS**, managed service, or paid API
- Any use that **commercializes the software** (not the same as running a commercial print business with the app internally)

---

## How to request a commercial license

Open a **private** GitHub issue or discussion on the project repository:

https://github.com/poitee/PrintPartnerPartner

Describe how you plan to use or distribute the software. Voluntary [Ko-fi tips](https://ko-fi.com/poitee) are appreciated but **do not** replace a commercial license.
